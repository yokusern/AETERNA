# 🚀 Social Media Strategy: Semi-Automated for $1000+/month

## ⚠️ Important: What NOT to Do

**❌ Don't use Twitter/X API for full automation:**
- Free tier: Only 500 posts/month (for testing only)
- Basic tier: $200/month (~¥30,000) - expensive!
- High risk of account suspension for link-heavy automated posting

---

## ✅ Recommended Strategy: Pinterest First

Based on research, **Pinterest + Gumroad** is the most effective combination:
- Proven results: $2,400/month (~¥360,000) achieved by creators
- Pinterest acts like a "visual search engine" - long-term traffic
- Perfect for digital products
- Lower risk of suspension

---

## 🎯 The Semi-Automated Workflow

### Step 1: Generate Products
```bash
# Create 5 products
python main.py --count 5
```

### Step 2: Generate Social Content
```bash
# Generate Pinterest Pins & Twitter tweets
python social_content_generator.py --count 5
```

### Step 3: Schedule Posts (Manual)
Copy-paste the generated content into scheduling tools:
- **Buffer** (free tier available)
- **Later** (great for Pinterest)
- **Tailwind** (Pinterest specialized)

---

## 📱 Content Breakdown

### Pinterest (Priority #1)
- **Frequency**: 5-10 pins/day
- **What to post**:
  - Eye-catching visuals (Canva templates work great)
  - Value-first descriptions
  - Link to Gumroad product
  - 5-10 relevant hashtags

### Twitter/X (Secondary)
- **Frequency**: 2-3 tweets/day
- **What to post**:
  - 70% value (tips, insights)
  - 30% promotion (product links)
  - Engage with others in your niche

### Reddit (Tertiary)
- **Frequency**: 1-2 posts/week
- **Rule**: 80% value, 20% promotion
- **Best subreddits**: r/sidehustle, r/passive_income, r/indiehackers
